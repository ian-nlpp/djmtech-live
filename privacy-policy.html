PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 22, 2025

Welcome to DJMTech! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how DJMTech (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our e-commerce website (the “Site”) and use our services (the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

a. Personal Information

When you create an account, place an order, or contact customer support, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party payment providers)

b. Automatically Collected Information

We collect certain data automatically when you interact with our Site, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Site
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

c. Transaction Information

Details related to your purchases, including order history and product preferences.

d. Customer Support Information

When you contact us, we collect details about your inquiry and correspondence.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about orders, updates, and promotions
  • To provide customer support and address inquiries
  • To personalize and improve your shopping experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party partners who assist with payment processing, shipping, customer support, and analytics.
  • Legal Compliance: Authorities if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or acquisition.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorized access, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@djmtech.com.

7. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our Site from outside the Philippines, note that your data may be processed in the Philippines or other countries with different data protection laws.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies before providing any information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@djmtech.com

Address: DJMTech, 123 Tech Avenue, Makati City, Metro Manila, Philippines
